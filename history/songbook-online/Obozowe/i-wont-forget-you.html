I won't forget you

Michał Makoś
I won't forget you!
I miss you when I see,
See you through my tears.
Your voice still in my ears.

  In my grief,
  Misery
  And my pain
  I fade!
  
  I'm going, I'm going,
  I'm going to you.

I won't forgive you!
I won't forgive myself,
That I cannot see your face
In that dark and foreign place.
